🚀 Escape Velocity

$$ v_{escape} = \sqrt{\frac{2GM}{R}} $$

जहाँ:

$v_{escape}$ = Escape velocity (पलायन वेग)

$G$ = Gravitational constant = $6.67 \times 10^{-11}$ N·m²/kg²

$M$ = Mass of planet (ग्रह का द्रव्यमान)

$R$ = Radius of planet (ग्रह की त्रिज्या)

Derivation:

Energy Conservation से:

$$ \frac{1}{2}mv^2 = \frac{GMm}{R} $$ $$ v = \sqrt{\frac{2GM}{R}} $$

⚡ Kinetic Energy

$$ KE = \frac{1}{2}mv^2 $$

Work-Energy Theorem:

$$ W = \Delta KE = KE_f - KE_i $$

$W$ = Work done (कार्य)

$m$ = Mass (द्रव्यमान) in kg

$v$ = Velocity (वेग) in m/s

$KE$ = Kinetic Energy in Joules

📊 Quadratic Formula

$$ x = \frac{-b \pm \sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}}{2a} $$

For equation: $ax^2 + bx + c = 0$

Discriminant: $\Delta = b^2 - 4ac$

• $\Delta > 0$ → दो real और distinct roots

• $\Delta = 0$ → एक repeated root

• $\Delta < 0$ → दो complex roots

🌊 Wave Equation

$$ v = f\lambda = \frac{\omega}{k} $$

Wave Properties:

$v$ = Wave velocity (तरंग वेग)

$f$ = Frequency (आवृत्ति) in Hz

$\lambda$ = Wavelength (तरंगदैर्ध्य) in meters

$\omega$ = Angular frequency = $2\pi f$

$k$ = Wave number = $\frac{2\pi}{\lambda}$

🔋 Ohm's Law & Power

$$ V = IR $$ $$ P = VI = I^2R = \frac{V^2}{R} $$

Electrical Quantities:

$V$ = Voltage (वोल्टेज) in Volts

$I$ = Current (धारा) in Amperes

$R$ = Resistance (प्रतिरोध) in Ohms (Ω)

$P$ = Power (शक्ति) in Watts
